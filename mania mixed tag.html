John Cena & Nikki Bella vs The Miz & Maryse confirmed for Wrestlemania 33

It was announced on March 14th's SmackDown that John Cena & Nikki Bella would face The Miz & Maryse in a mixed tag team match at WrestleMania. That match had been expected for weeks and Dave Meltzer reported it was the plan in the February 6th edition of the Wrestling Observer Newsletter.

Miz and Maryse continued to verbally attack Cena and Bella on SmackDown before the announcement, which brought the faces out to the ring. Daniel Bryan then made the match after Miz and Maryse tried to back away from confrontation.

There is now nine matches confirmed for Wrestlemania 33 which will take place on April 2nd in Orlando, Florida

The 'feud' started on two fronts a few weeks ago. Nikki was competing in a falls count anywhere match against Natalya when the fight ended up going backstage and Nikki was thrown into Maryse.
