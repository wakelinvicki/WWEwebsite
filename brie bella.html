Brie Bella

As one-half of the dazzling Bella Twins, Brie debuted on SmackDown in 2008, defeating her first opponent, Victoria, by way of “Twin Magic” — a move in which she and her identical sister, Nikki, secretly switched places during matches. In 2011, the sneaky tactic helped Brie defeat Eve to win the Divas Championship.

Outside the ring, Nikki & Brie have thrived as the center pieces of the hit E! reality show “Total Divas,” alongside the likes of Paige, Natalya, Eva Marie, Summer Rae, Rosa Mendes and Alicia Fox. Life as a reality star didn’t dull Brie’s abilities in the ring; alongside sister Nikki, she quickly staked out a spot at the top of her division. Even though she has retired from in-ring competition, “Brie Mode” will be the default status on the WWE Universe’s lips for a long, long time to come.
