Tamina

Hit like a girl? Try telling that to Tamina, a stunning combination of a WWE Hall of Fame lineage and unrelenting ruthless aggression who is more than capable of running rabid throughout the women's division.

Featuring overwhelming size and strength to go along with the ability to fly off the top rope, Tamina is a force to be reckoned with. When not overpowering her opponents in singles competition, Tamina takes full advantage of her intimidating presence while acting as the muscle behind other elite female Superstars.

So, go ahead. Tell her she’s tough “for a woman,” but be ready to apologize once she’s done with you.
