"The Golden Goddess"

When you’re 5-foot-4 and can lift 225 pounds, you’re bound to turn some heads. An athletic powerhouse who stays in shape with dance, yoga, Pilates and CrossFit, Mandy Rose's physical prowess and brash attitude make her a welcome addition to both WWE NXT and E!'s "Total Divas."

The vivacious Mandy got her start on the 2015 edition of WWE Tough Enough where she was the runner-up for the female crown. Since then, she has taken "Total Divas" by storm, forging a bond with Eva Marie. And although Mandy has yet to step foot in a WWE ring, she has already more than proven to have the mettle, and the toughness, to hang with the best of the best.
